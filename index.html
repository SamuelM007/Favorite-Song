Can't Hold Us

by Macklemore & Ryan Lewis

"Can't Hold Us" by Macklemore & Ryan Lewis first came out in 2011, but it really blew up when they included it on their album "The Heist" in 2012. Featuring Ray Dalton, the song became a huge hit and even reached number one on the Billboard Hot 100, showing off the duo's awesome energy and style.
